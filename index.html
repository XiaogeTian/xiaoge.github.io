Biography
Xiaoge Tian (田晓歌) is a second-year Ph.D. student at HKU SAIL supervised by Prof. Jiaji Wang. Previously, he worked with Prof. Chen at China University of Geosciences(Wuhan). His research goal covers the whole pipeline of physics-informed machine learning: (1) Physics-informed neural network, (2) Physics-informed reinforcement learning, and (3) Deep reinforecement learning with operation research. Name

Family name: 田(Mǎ) means field.

Given name: 晓(Píng)歌(chuān) means the morning and dawn with labors' singing.

Quoted from a Chinese idiom 清晨放歌, my full name means singing songs in the morning freely and happily.

春眠不觉晓，处处闻啼鸟晓晨平川。

宋·孟浩然 《春晓》

Sleeping in on a spring morn — sensing not the dawn

Meng Haoran of the Tang dynasty in Relaxing at home
